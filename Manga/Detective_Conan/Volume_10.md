#### File 092 - The Great Detective of the West
When Conan returns home, a teenager is at Kogoro's office, demanding to see Shinichi. He asks Ran about Shinichi's latest updates, and based on Ran's information, he concludes that Shinichi is monitoring her from somewhere because Shinichi only talks about himself whenever he calls.

Later, the teenager introduces himself as Heiji Hattori, a high school detective from Osaka. Heiji explains that although Shinichi rarely appears in the newspaper recently, he wants to meet Shinichi to find out if Shinichi is really a comparable detective. He offers a drink to Conan as a cold remedy, which turns out to be a Chinese liquor. He gives the liquor to Kogoro as a gift and tells them that he will be staying there until he sees Shinichi. Ran protests with Heiji's idea, but a client comes into the office and interrupts her. The client is Kimie Tsujimura, a diplomat's wife who comes with a request that Kogoro investigate her son's girlfriend, Yukiko Katsuragi. Yukiko is a 24-year-old medical student who is going to become a doctor. Kogoro finds no fault with Yukiko, but before Kimie explains her problem, Heiji replies that Yukiko is too perfect and Kimie is looking for any flaws. Kimie suggests to Kogoro that he come to her house and discuss the matter with her husband, Isao, in a low-profile meeting because Isao is a diplomat. Heiji convinces them to bring him along to avoid suspicions and asks Ran to come as well, hoping Shinichi might show up if Ran goes along. Conan wants to tag along in order to keep an eye on Heiji despite his cold worsening after the alcoholic drink.

When they arrive at Kimie's residence, Kimie asks the butler, Fumio, where her husband is, and he tells her that Isao is in the study room upstairs. Yukiko suddenly appears, calling Kimie a mother, which angers Kimie more. Kimie wonders why she is there, and Isao's son, Takayoshi, says that he invited her to the house to meet Isao. Takayoshi is not happy with Kimie for scolding Yukiko, revealing that Kimie is the second wife of his father. Kimie leads the guest to the study room and is surprised with the sudden appearance of her father-in-law, Toshimitsu. Toshimitsu claims that Kimie was the one who invited him over to share the story about the fish he caught. She asks him to wait in another room and later reveals to the guests that Toshimitsu is experiencing poor memory recently and will have forgotten about the meeting.

Kimie knocks on the study room's door but receives no responses from the inside. She uses the key to open the door and finds Isao apparently sleeping on the chair while the radio is playing opera music. When Kimie tries to wake him up, he falls from the chair, and everyone is shocked.

#### File 093 - Two Heroes / Double Reasoning
Heiji pronounces Isao dead, and the rest of the household arrive at the room after Kimie's scream. Kogoro warns everyone not to enter the study room until the police arrive. He also informs the rest of the household about Isao's death. Conan finds Isao's body is still warm and his lips are turning purple. Then, Conan sees a needle nearby and wants to examine it but accidentally bumps his head against Heiji. Heiji tells Conan that a kid like him should stay away from the dead body. 

Before Conan could tell Inspector Megure about Isao's cause of death, Heiji interjects and explains in detail how someone killed Isao with a poisoned needle. Heiji also estimates that Isao was killed 30 minutes before they entered the study room. Megure wonders who Heiji is, and after Kogoro reveals Heiji's full name, Megure recognises Heiji as the son of Heizo Hattori, Osaka Prefecture's chief of police. Heiji appears displeased when Megure mentions his father and asks Megure to check for any signs of forced entry. Heiji's analysis of the case immediately reminds Ran of Shinichi. Megure checks the room and finds out that all the windows are locked from the inside, indicating that someone entered the room with a spare key. Kimie tells Megure that there are only two keys; one is with her and another is with the husband. Megure checks Isao's pant pocket and finds the key is still in the pocket. Heiji deduces that the murderer locked the door when leaving the room and declares that the case is a "locked-room murder case". Heiji notices Kogoro is surprised by the revelation. Heiji believes that Shinichi was the one who solved all the previous cases, and Kogoro secretly seeks advice from Shinichi. Heiji is excited with the finding, and in his mind, he's thinking of solving the case before Shinichi does. Meanwhile, Conan is having a heavy flu and wants to solve the case fast so that he can go home to rest. Heiji suggests Megure check the suspects for their alibis, and Megure asks Kogoro not to lose to Heiji in solving this case.

Based on the given alibis, Heiji believes that Toshimitsu, Takoyashi, and Yukiko are the most suspicious. Megure inspects the room and finds a collection of classical music, although the opera music was playing at the time when Isao died. Megure also sees a photo of Isao and Kimie from 20 years ago, but Conan can't see it clearly because he has blurry vision. An officer asks Megure what to do with the books, which reminds Conan of the existence of books stacked near the body. Another officer informs Megure that Isao's keyholder slides into two halves, and a Sellotape with a bump is inside. Conan tries to see, but Ran grabs his collar and asks him not to disturb Heiji. Conan is confused and notices Ran is smiling as she watches Heiji. Conan has a chest spasm and wants to solve the case fast and get rid of Heiji. Heiji figures out something and suddenly dashes out of the room. At the same time. Conan also finds out the trick to the locked room, but before he can reveal who the murderer is, he collapses in the study room.

Ran accuses Shinichi of telling lies and believes that he is watching her nearby and laughing at her for being worried. ### Case 026 - [Library Murder Case](https://detectiveconan96.fandom.com/wiki/Volume_10#Library_Murder_Case)
The Detective Boys come to the house, and they go to the library to help Conan finish a book report he couldn't complete while he was sick. Initially, Conan is not interested in spending time with the kids, but once he thinks that it could be the last time to hang out with them if he transforms back to Shinichi, he goes ahead with their plan.

At the library, Conan notices the books in the kids' section lack organisation and later discovers a box of individually wrapped foreign books. Shuji Tsugawa, the library director, suddenly appears and tells Conan that the books are not suitable for children. Genta calls Conan, and they join the others who are looking at policemen arriving at the library. Conan dashes out to investigate, followed by others. They try to take the lift, but it exceeds the maximum capacity of passengers, causing them to have to rush down using the stairs. Megure asks Shuji about a missing employee named Kazuo Yamada. Shuji tells him that Kazuo has been absent since yesterday. Megure explains that Kazuo's wife told the police that he has been missing since two nights ago. Shuji doesn't know where Kazuo is because he left the library before Kazuo when they were working together a few nights ago. Megure explains to Conan that Kazuo always calls his wife before going home, but he didn't do that two nights ago. Megure deduces that Kazuo was either abducted while he was working in the library or he'd already been murdered. The police leave after they thoroughly search the library without finding anything.

After the library closes, Conan and the Detective Boys sneak out of hiding. Conan plans to look for the corpse due to finding the library suspicious. Before they can begin, the lift becomes in use. The kids hide, and Conan sees Shuji arrive and walks to the box of foreign books from earlier that day. Shuji monologues to himself about killing Kazuo for finding the books and tricking the police by brilliantly hiding the body in the library. This confirms Conan's suspicions about the corpse, but he has no idea where it is hidden.

Conan suggests calling the police, but when they reach the first-floor lobby, the phone is disconnected. Initially, Conan prefers to leave to inform the police directly, but the others want to find the body for additional achievements. 

The library has four floors, and they plan to start from the first. Suddenly, Ayumi accidentally steps on her shoelace and stops for a while to put her shoe back. Tsuji appears from behind, preparing to kill her before Conan turns back and calls Ayumi. They can't find the corpse after looking at every bookshelf around the library. Genta finds it difficult to move one of the sliding bookshelves, which is actually caused by his body weight. ### Case 027 - Medical Professors Murder Case / Snowy Mountain Cabin Case
#### File 099 - Tragedy in the Blizzard / Snowstorm Tragedy
Kogoro, Ran, and Conan have a vacation at a snowy mountain after Conan won the free accommodation at a raffle. After skiing, Kogoro only finds out that he lost the key to the rental cabin. There is no phone around to call the manager of the lodge, and other cabins are under construction. Ran suggests asking for help from the private lodge nearby, but Kogoro despises asking for help from wealthy people. Then, a car passes by; Professor Masashi Oyama, who admires Kogoro after reading about his detective skills in newspapers, invites them to come to the lodge to make a call. When they arrive at Masashi's lodge, Kaori is just returning from shopping. They are planning to have a hot pot and invite Kogoro, Ran, and Conan to join them. 

During the hotpot, Ran is fascinated that Masashi's guests are doctors from Tohto University Medical School who work in his lab. The guests are as follows:
* Tomoyasu Kanazawa - Associate Professor
* Ginji Tobita - Researcher
* Kaho Ezumi - Researcher
* Kaori Nakahara - Researcher

Ginji mentions that if Masashi wins the next dean election, all of them will have a bright future, thanks to Masashi's paper. Suddenly, Kaori realises that she forgot to buy snacks for the saké. She plans to go to the convenience store at the foot of the mountain to get the snacks. After saying goodbye to Kaori at the doorstep, Masashi asks Kaho for the time. Kaho replies that it's 8.58, and Masashi quickly rushes to the TV. Kaho tells Ran that there is a soap drama at 9 pm on Saturdays, and Masashi is hooked on it. Ran suggests watching it with Masashi, but Kaho says that Masashi always watches alone in the office and gets angry if anyone disturbs him. Meanwhile, Ginji is going for night skiing, and Tomoyasu plans to take a relaxing bath until Kaori comes back with the snacks. 

Conan suddenly remembers about the call to the lodge's manager, and Ran plans to make a call immediately before Kaho stops her. Kaho asks them to spend the night there, plus Kogoro is in a heavily drunk state. Ran and Conan help to wash the dishes while Kaho takes the trash out. From the kitchen's window, Ran and Conan see a heavy blizzard outside.

In the living room, Masashi is completely engrossed in the TV drama, unaware of the unlocked windows. Kaori returns with the snacks, and Ginji comes back in freezing cold. While everyone is enjoying the snacks and saké, Conan wonders where Masashi is since the show has already ended. The others say that Masashi always records the show and watches the good scenes several times. Meanwhile, another scene shows that Masashi is tied up with ropes and stabbed with a knife. He is talking to someone, mentioning that the unknown person purposely brings him to the lodge. He plans to leave a message for Kogoro and believes the person won't be able to escape because the great detective is there. 

Conan asks where Masashi is because it's already 11 pm. Kaori goes to the living room to check on Masashi, and later, everyone in the other room hears her scream. They quickly run to the living room and find Masashi is dead.

#### File 100 - The Last Word
Kogoro, who is still drunk, warns everyone not to enter the room until the police arrive. Ran tells Kogoro that the police and the ambulance can't reach there due to the blizzard. 

Kogoro asks whether there are any valuables in the room, and Tomoyasu replies that there is a safe in the cabinet to the right of the TV. Kogoro figures the safe door is scratched up, while Conan finds bloody tracks on the carpet which look like the victim crawled to the wall. While Conan is thinking about the strange bloody rope marks on the tablecloth, Kogoro hits Conan and asks him to stay out of it. Kogoro notices the windows are open and deduces that the murderer snuck in through the windows, leaving his footprints outside. Since there is no other option, Kogoro asks the guests for help by performing an autopsy on Masashi without tainting the scene.

Conan finds a bloody lighter on the sofa. The bloody side was face down, yet there was no blood on the sofa. Suddenly, Conan remembers the bloody tablecloth and finds a perfect spot where the lighter was originally placed. Conan recalls the victim mentioning that a horse would have been nice for the door when Ran and Conan introduced themselves. Conan believes that the victim left behind a dying message, and he knows who the murderer is.
