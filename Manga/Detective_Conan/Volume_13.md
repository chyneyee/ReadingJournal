# Detective Conan Volume 13 - Life's A Beach... Then You Get Murdered!

## Summary

### Case 033 - [Holmes Freak Case / Holmes Enthusiasts Murder Case](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_13#Holmes_Freak_Case) 
#### File 121 - The Revelation / Real Self

<details>
  <summary>Spoiler</summary>

Conan uses Heiji to declare Kento Togano as the murderer. The owner was placed in the car with a foot holding the brake, but when the rigor mortis disappeared, the foot on the brake relaxed, and the car started moving on its own. The hot temperature outside quickens the rigor mortis, and the sound in the car was the heater, not the air conditioner. The blanket on the dashboard is meant to prevent anyone from seeing the heater is on.

Ayako lit her lighter to find the book in the back seat of the car. The flame ignites the gasoline vapours and sets off the fire. Kento panicked when Ayako figured out that he was the killer. Kento told Ayako that the first-edition book was in the garage. However, Conan clarifies that Kento's garage plan was not intended to kill Ayako but Toshiaki. Since the plan didn't go well and killed Ayako, Kento came up with another plan to cause a blackout and attacked Toshiaki. Ayako knew that Kento was the culprit because she decided to cheat on her Holmes test and went to Kento's room, but Kento wasn't there. Ayako saw the test sheet was blank. Kento wasn't concerned with the test because he knew the owner wouldn't be alive to grade it.

Kento confesses and reveals Toshiaki and Hiroyuki published a book that had Irene Adler belittle Holmes. After the revelation, Heiji wakes up and Ran praises him for being the great detective of the West. Heiji later reveals to Conan that he was spying on Conan during the deduction. Conan continues denying it until Heiji threatens to tell Ran the truth. Heiji knows the entire story about the Black Organisation and Conan's identity. Conan makes a bad comment about Ran, and Heiji tells it to Ran as revenge for putting him to sleep.
