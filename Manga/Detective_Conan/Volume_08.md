#### File 071 - Found At Last !!
<details>
  <summary>Spoiler</summary>

  Ran, Conan, and Ryoko rush to Naoki's house to save Mamoru. Conan has made a call to Dr Agasa for backup. 

  Naoki hesitates to open the door and gives the excuse that his girlfriend is inside. Ran uses her karate skill to kick the door. They find Mamoru unhurt and playing video games there. They quickly make a call to Hideo to tell him that Mamoru is safe. 

  Naoki has been treating Hideo as his rival since high school. He felt he was better than Hideo until Hideo shattered his right leg in a practice game soon after he joined the team. He feels Hideo aimed for his leg on purpose. He was out for three months, and he couldn't catch up to Hideo's progress. Naoki mentions that he never planned to hurt Mamoru. He only wants to prove to Hideo that life doesn't always go as planned. 

  The Tokyo Spirits wins after Hideo scores two goals. Naoki asks Ryoko to call the police to report on him. After the match ends, a reporter interviews Hideo, and he tells him that he dedicates the match to Naoki and wishes Naoki a speedy recovery. Naoki feels guilty about it and says Hideo is an idiot because Hideo doesn't know what he has done.

  While Naoki is feeling guilty about his action, Ran suddenly appears and asks Naoki where Shinichi is hiding. Ryoko explains that actually, she doesn't know him and gives Ran Shinichi's fax number. Conan dashes out because the cell phone is in his backpack, but Ran thought Shinichi was the one who ran out of the apartment. 

Conan couldn't open the backpack and hide behind a wall. Ran questions him about who Ryoko is and why she was crying when talking on the phone with him. Ran says that she feels like crying. Conan stands on top of the pallet in front of a car's headlights, casting Shinichi's shadow on the wall. Shinichi says that he's innocent and asks Ran not to cry because he doesn't like to see her cry. Shinichi runs away after that, and Ran chases after him. Doctor Agasa is in the car and tells Ran that Shinichi ran away with a blushing face. Conan hides in the backseat of the car.

Ran makes Ryoko repeat her explanation of the case several times, which she kept from the police. Ran finally believes in Shinichi's innocence after three days.
</details>
