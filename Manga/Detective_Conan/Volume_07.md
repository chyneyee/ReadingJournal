# Detective Conan Volume 7 - The Curse of the Moonlight Sonata

## Summary
### Case 017 - [Tenkaichi Festival Case](https://detectiveconan96.fandom.com/wiki/Volume_7#Tenkaichi_Festival_Case)
#### File 061 - Caught on Film / The Photo Trap
<details>
  <summary>Spoiler</summary>
  
According to the witnesses, the man came out less than one minute after the gunshot. The man doesn't have enough time to look for the wallet and ransack the room. This man purposely created the mess before the murder to appear as a robbery. 

Sasai took the first photo when he came to the festival in the past. Every photo was taken in front of Conan, Ran, and Kogoro except the first photo. He has to endure the heat at the festival because he is wearing the same clothes as in the photo. Conan has the proof that the first photo was taken in the past. Before he can reveal the proof, Ran catches Conan hiding behind her father. Luckily, Detective Yokomizo figures out the difference between the two photos. The first photo shows a pale strip on Sasai's wrist, which was the watch's mark. The alibi's photo doesn't have the mark. 

Sasai admits that he took the photo a year ago, which coincided with the time he planned to kill Imatake. One year ago, Sasai was chosen to write the main serial in the Literary Art monthly. Unfortunately, the editorial department dropped him after they got Imatake because he was more well-known. Since then, Sasai's career sank lower. Lastly, Sasai mentions the piece that made Imatake win the Naomoto Award was a collaborative idea between Imatake and Sasai.
