#### File 081 - A Dangerous Game of Hide and Seek
The Junior Detective League or Detective Boys (Conan, Ayumi, Genta, and Mitsuhiko) are playing hide-and-seek. Ayumi hides in the car's trunk, and without her knowledge, the car leaves the park after that.

Conan, Genta, and Mitsuhiko couldn't find Ayumi in the park. Conan uses the detective badge, which has a built-in radio, to locate her. When Ayumi wakes up from her nap, she realises that she's in a car. She finds a bag of cash and a saw. When Ayumi finds a head in the trunk, she screams, which alerts the two men in front of the car.

<br>

#### File 082 - Follow the Voice / Follow That Voice!!
Ayumi screams and tells the boys that she finds a girl's head inside the trunk. Conan suspects the car belongs to the serial killer whom he saw in the news earlier. The two men hear the scream but have doubts about what they heard. When they decide to check the trunk, they drive past the amusement park, leading them to assume that the scream comes from there. Ayumi bursts into tears, but Conan convinces her that he will come to save her.

Conan uses the skateboard given by Dr Agasa. The turbo-engine skateboard only can be used in daylight, as it uses solar power. Conan uses the homing glasses to track Ayumi since Ayumi's badge has a transmitter, but after reaching a certain place, the glasses are on low battery. Ayumi complains that the car is moving and stopping and also has weird noises outside. Conan asks her to increase the volume on the badge's radio, and he figures out that the car is stuck in construction traffic.

Suddenly, they see several police cars nearby. There is news reporting nearby about the police tracking the two men who are suspects for the serial killer kidnapping and murder case. Conan asks Ayumi whether she hears any police sirens, and she replies that she heard about three police cars pass by. Conan's team plans to wait at the Beika bridge, but the car never appears. They can't receive Ayumi's response from the badge, wondering whether she is out of the range of the radio waves. Conan assumes the only possibility is that the car is inside a building that blocks the radio waves.

All of a sudden, they hear Ayumi shouting to them. Ayumi tells them that before she lost transmission with Conan earlier, she heard a "whoosh" sound. Conan thinks that the car has passed the checkpoint, and he is hoping that he can chase the car while the skateboard is still working. Ayumi hears a machine and water sound, which makes them believe that the car is in a petrol station. Conan hears the petrol station attendant is telling the two men about a special offer, and Conan remembers which petrol station is doing the Kamen Yaiber promotion. Conan tracks the car, and it passes them after the car leaves the petrol station.

<br>

#### File 083 - What! Really?
Conan is unsure whether Ayumi is in the car that they're following. While they're following the car from behind, Conan takes an empty can, throws it at the car's trunk and asks whether Ayumi hears it. Ayumi confirms that she heard something hit the car. Conan has to stop the car before the skateboard dies. Conan sees someone watering the plants on the pavement. He grabs the water hose and ties it to the car's side mirror. Conan asks the driver to stop, but the driver continues to drive and removes the hose from the side mirror. Conan then grabs the hose and ties it to the car's antenna on top of the car's trunk.

### Case 023 - [Kogoro's Class Reunion](https://detectiveconan96.fandom.com/wiki/Volume_9#Kogoro's_Class_Reunion)
#### File 084 - Kogoro's Class Reunion
Kogoro meets his old classmates at Beiki Inn, a hot spring inn in Tochigi Prefecture. Ran is having a hot spring bath outside while Kogoro is talking to his friends inside the inn. Kogoro's classmate teases Kogoro for rarely coming to the judo practice when they were in university, resulting in him never winning an official match. Ran comes in and exposes Kogoro's lie, who always brags about how "unbeatable" he was in college. Later, it reveals Conan was taking a bath with Ran, causing him to have a nosebleed. Kogoro guesses Conan is having a nosebleed because he saw Ran naked, but Ran denies it and treats Conan as a kid. Conan thinks Ran would kill him if Ran knew who he is.

The following are Kogoro's friends who attend the reunion: -

* Yukio Ayashiro – the champion of the judo team and a high school P.E. teacher.
* Jun Omura – the team's best clutch wrestler and a model shop manager.
* Yumi Horikoshi – the judo team's beautiful manager and a cosmetic company employee.
* Noriko Kimoto – Yukio's wife.
* Kazushi Nakamichi – the captain of the judo team and a detective from Chiba Police.

While looking at the photo album, they remember that Yumi used to date Kazushi, leading Yumi to tell them that she had already moved on and revealing Kazushi is getting married. Yumi suddenly gets depressed with her single life and uncertain future in her work. Everyone is worried about her until she claims that was a joke. She even jokingly asks Jun to marry her since both of them are single, but before Jun wants to explain further, she says it was a joke.

When everyone plans to play some ping pong before the fireworks, Yumi is unhappy with the plan and decides to return to her room. Kogoro and Jun agree that Yumi doesn't like being told what to do. During the ping pong game, Jun goes to the bathroom while Ran is talking to Kazushi. Kazushi won the national championship before, but he denies he was the strongest because there was one person stronger than him. Before he reveals who the person is, Jun comes back and looks sad. Kazushi asks Jun what happened, but Jun replies, 'Nothing.' Conan looks confused by it.

Later, when Jun, Kogoro, Ran, and Conan are rushing to the fireworks area, Ran suddenly remembers Yumi and plans to return to the inn to wake her up. Ran knocks on the room's door but receives no response. Then, Kazushi appears and advises them not to wake Yumi up because she gets in a bad mood if someone disrupts her sleep. They are surprised that Kazushi is still in the inn, and he tells them that he stays back to take a bath. Then, Yukio and Noriko appear, and they walk to the fireworks area together.

At the fireworks area, Ran and Conan see Kogoro and Jun. When Kogoro asks where the others are, and only then Ran finds out that Kazushi, Yukio and Noriko are not with them. Back in the inn, Kogoro is upset that his friends didn't turn up after he and Jun got a good spot. Conan asks whether Yumi is still sleeping, and Kazushi suggests everyone goes to Yumi's room to wake her up. When they arrive at the room, they are shocked to find out that Yumi is dead.

<br>

#### File 085 - An Unexpected Hint
Kogoro examines Yumi's body and finds a bullet wound to the temple and a gun in her right hand. It's too early to conclude that it's a suicide, and they decide to wait for the police to arrive. Then, Ran appears and tells him that the police need two hours to arrive due to the festivals in the area, leading Kogoro to decide to do a postmortem. When Kogoro is examining Yumi's body, Conan notices that Yumi is wearing a large bathrobe, but she was wearing a smaller one in the lobby. Her hands appear stiff around the gun, and her hand isn't on the trigger. Kazushi claims that the rigor mortis indicates that Yumi has been dead for over seven hours. Kogoro deduces that she must have committed suicide after she went back to her room in the afternoon. However, after Conan asks Kogoro how Yumi shot herself, Kogoro finds out that there aren't burn marks on her head, which should exist if the gun were pressed to the head. The new finding makes Kogoro believe that she was shot by someone and tried to make it look like a suicide.

Kazushi believes an outsider snuck into the room from the roof outside the window. Meanwhile, Jun looks anxious, which leads Kogoro to believe that he is hiding something. At first, Jun denies it, but later, he reveals that he saw Yumi when he went to the bathroom in the afternoon. Yumi's time of death was predicted at 3 pm, while Jun left for the bathroom after five. Jun claims he saw Yumi glaring down at the ping pong room from a window. Everyone thinks what Juin said is impossible because she is already dead at that time. 

Kogoro knows the murder was not committed by an outsider and believes that only his friends are capable of making the murder look like a suicide. While Conan is still thinking about the findings from the body examination, he passes by the Benkei statue. The statue has all arrows embedded in it, which depicts the famous fear of Benkei dying on his feet. Conan suddenly figures out what the murderer was trying to do.

#### File 086 - The Two Benkei Standing Deaths
<details>
<summary>Spoiler</summary>

Conan finds out the murderer's trick and plans to use the stun-gun wristwatch on Kogoro. Before Conan does so, he remembers Kogoro was extremely upset because one of his friends is the killer, and Kogoro promised that he would wrestle out the truth. Conan suggests playing ping pong, and Ran stops him from making such a suggestion at such a time, but actually, Conan is leaving a few hints of the case for Kogoro. 

Kogoro deduces from the provided clues and reveals that Kazushi is the killer. Kazushi denies the crime because he was at the ping pong room when the murder occurred. Kogoro explains that the rigor mortis can be affected if the sudden death occurs during vigorous exercise, which causes proteins in the muscles to harden easily and quickens the rigor mortis process. Kogoro further deduces that Kazushi arranged to meet Yumi alone in the ping pong room, but instead, he invited everyone to play ping pong. After everyone left the inn around six, Yumi went to the ping pong room and played with Kazushi. After that, Kazushi got her to return to her room and shot her in the head. Yumi was probably still holding her racquet when she went back to the room, which explains why her right hand had stiffened in the shakehand grip. Yumi's real time of death was probably around 6.30 pm. Kazushi chose to fire the gun at the time the fireworks had already started. Kazushi later wiped her sweat and changed her clothes with the spare bathrobe in the room. However, Ran and Conan unexpectedly interrupted him when they went to wake Yumi up. In the panic, Kazushi climbed out the window into the hallway to appear as if he were coming back from a bath. During the fireworks, he pretended to be lost in the crowd and returned to the inn to place the gun in Yumi's hand, but the shakehand grip hand had already stiffened, which caused the finger to be unable to be moved onto the trigger. 

Conan offers another hint to Kogoro when Kazushi is asking for proof. Kogoro remembers Kazushi asked to call for the police instead of an ambulance when they found Yumi. It indicates that Kazushi knew that Yumi was too late to be saved. This prompts Jun to attack Kazushi and shout at him about why he killed Yumi. Kazushi throws Jun onto the ground and reveals that he dated Yumi for eighteen years until six months before he met his current fiancée. Then, Yukio tries to fight with Kazushi after asking why he didn't marry Yumi. Kazushi tells them that he proposed to Yumi so many times, but she rejected him during the eighteen years they were together. When Kazushi was going to marry someone else, Yumi threatened him with not allowing him to be happy. Yumi harassed his fiancée with letters and phone calls and threatened to send her a picture of them. Kazushi suggested to Yumi that she bring all the pictures to the reunion, and he would buy them from her. The planned exchange was to be in the ping pong room, but she said the photos were in her room and demanded to be paid first. Later, he said to her that he already took the photos out of her luggage, which made Yumi hurry back to the room and gave Kazushi the opportunity to kill her. Kazushi accuses Yumi of being the demon who ruined his life, and Kogoro questions who is the real demon. Kazushi grasps Kogoro's robe, and Kogoro throws Kazushi on the floor and tells him that he's not interested in knowing how a murderer thinks.

Three days later, Kogoro is still upset with what happened. Conan wonders why Kogoro never wins any matches if he were that strong. Ran assumes her father probably got so tense in real matches and couldn't concentrate in them. Conan concludes in his mind that Kogoro was weak under pressure. Later, Kogoro's mood gets better after seeing a woman's skirt blow up in the wind.

### Case 024 - Wealthy Daughter Murder Case
#### File 087 - Groom Selection / Choosing a Groom
Kogoro is invited as a special guest to a party for the celebration of the 24th birthday for Reika Yotsui, the only daughter of the Yotsui Group's chairman.

The guests who introduce themselves to Kogoro are as follows:
- Takashi Ichieda, City Bank main branch fund procurement division.
- Yuji Nikaido, AD agency sales department.
- Takuya Mifune, President of Mifune Electronics.
- Osamu Gojo, Gojo Trading adjutant general.
- Masashi Rokuda, Director of Yotsui & Co. Ltd.

While some of the guests are discussing Reika, Conan is trying to find the bathroom in the villa. When he finds one, the lights are not working. Yone Nanao, the housekeeper for the Yotsui family, suggests he use the other bathroom. Yone comments that it's strange that the lights are out in the hallway, the toilet, and the bath.

When Mr Yotsui decides to call it a night, the chauffeur comes into the hall and informs Mr Yotsui that the car that he drove Reika in has punctured tyres. Immediately, the male guests offer to drive Reika back home, but their cars also have punctured tyres. Mr Yotsui suggests the daughter get into one of the strangers' cars, but she refuses and decides to spend the night in the villa. The male guests choose to stay behind after hearing Reika's decision to stay, and Masashi tells Mr Yotsui that he stays behind to protect Reika. When the male guests try to flatter Erika, Conan hears Yuji mention that Reika's fiancé was already chosen two years ago.

Meanwhile, Kogoro is too drunk to drive, and Takuya offers to drive three of them home. Reika asks whether Takuya is planning to leave, reminding him that he is one of the candidates to be her groom. Takuya replies that he came for the business deals between two companies, and he has no interest in her. This angers Reika, and she tosses the car key away with a threat to end the deal with Takuya's company if he leaves.

Later, Reika goes upstairs to change her clothes while the rest are playing cards. During the card game, Ran finds out the male guests are from the same yacht club. They reminisce about the old times until Takuya mentions an accident that occurred two years ago, but they don't want to talk more about it. Then, Masashi asks what is taking Reika so long. The guests are getting worried and go around the house to look for her. They couldn't find her in the house, and Kogoro suggests splitting up and looking for her in the forest. During the search, the rain suddenly starts to pour. Kogoro, Ran, and Conan head back to the villa and find Yuji dead in the fountain.

<br>

#### File 088 - The Encroaching Shadow / The Creeping Shadow
Kogoro deduces that Yuji was probably forced into the fountain and drowned to death. Takuya accuses Reika of being the murderer, but Kogoro says that it's too early to reach the conclusion and suggests calling the police. Unfortunately, the phones are not working, and Kogoro's car has punctured tyres too. Masashi suggests taking a break to think about their next step and plans to get some towels for everyone. Conan comments that he seems very familiar with the house, and Masashi responds that it's because the villa was a gathering place for the yacht club.

Meanwhile, Ran is helping the housekeeper to make coffee in the kitchen. When the housekeeper asks Ran to help find the sugar on the top shelf, a mysterious person puts something in the coffee. 

The male guests are discussing Yuji's death, and Conan asks them what happened two years ago. Kogoro thinks it's irrelevant, prompting Conan to explain to them about what Yuji said regarding the fiancé having already decided two years ago. Masashi begins to tell how the accident occurred, which relates to their yacht sailing on a stormy day. Reika was left alone on the island while the rest were evacuated to the cruiser. One of them took an inflatable boat to look for her, but later, Reika was found with Yuji floating in the ocean wearing life jackets. The corpse of the person who went to look for her was discovered three days later, and the victim is Yone's granddaughter, Yaeko. 

Kogoro suggests splitting into two groups to look for Reika. Ran feels sleepy after drinking the coffee and lying down on the sofa. Yone misses her granddaughter when looking at Ran sleeping while the rest try to look for Reika around the house. Then, a blackout suddenly occurs, and Kogoro, Takashi, and Conan rush down to the hallway to search for the circuit breaker. Conan hears some crashing and glass-shattering sounds when the lights are on; everyone sees a broken window in the hallway. Takuya and Takashi run to the forest to catch the perpetrator, and the rest are chasing after them. When Kogoro and Conan stop to catch their breath, they find out Yone is also there, leading Conan to realise that Ran is alone in the house. 

Meanwhile, in the house, a mysterious person is carrying the motionless body of Ran to the kitchen. Ran is unable to move her body to fight with the mysterious person who is putting her head into an overflowing basin.

<br>

#### File 089 - Another Body...
Conan rushes back to the house and looks for Ran. The mysterious person smirks before he flees. Conan tries to wake Ran up, but in her confused state, she imagines Shinichi is there when she hears Conan's voice and falls back to sleep. Conan wonders whether there is a sleeping pill in the coffee and realises that the microwave is warm. 

Takuya and Masashi decide to lock themselves in the room while waiting for a car to pick them up in three hours. The others stay together in the living room while Conan is still wondering why someone tried to attack Ran. 

Yone makes another round of coffee around 7 in the morning. Kogoro looks hesitant with the coffee. Takashi casually slaps Kogoro's shoulder and jokingly says that the coffee is not spiked with sleeping pills. The coffee spills on both Kogoro and Conan. They rush to the bathroom, and when they reach there, they see Reika's head is on the overflowing bathtub.

<br>

#### File  090 - A Random Murder / Indiscriminate Killings
Reika was drowned to death by someone forcing her head into the tub. There are rope marks on her hands and legs and signs of duct tape on her mouth. She was probably confined somewhere until the right time to be killed. Kogoro deduces that Reika has been dead at least one or two hours. Takuya and Masashi are the main suspects because the others were in the parlour when they shut themselves in the room. In the bathroom, Conan checks on the taps of the bathtub and turns it on. The tap is on the shower setting, and he finds out how the murderer misled them. 


Everyone decides to go to the parlour and wait for the car to arrive. Conan uses the stun-gun wristwatch on Kogoro to put him to sleep and the voice modulator to change to Kogoro's voice. He figures out a way to find the murderer and asks them to remove their jackets. One of their shirts has some water blotches, which reveals who the murderer is.

<summary>Manga vs Anime - Reference from the Site</summary>

  * In the manga version, Reika announces to her father and the guests that she intends to marry one of the suitors at the party. This scene is removed in the anime.
  * Reika wears a white coat over her dress when she is about to leave the house with her father after the party has ended in the anime.
  * Some of Reika's expressions and mannerisms were changed to be less malevolent, leaning more towards being childish, compared to the manga.
  * In the anime, after Yone puts a blanket on Ran, who is sleeping on a sofa, she looks at Ran's face and is reminded of her granddaughter Yaeko.
  * In the anime, Ichieda doesn't drink the coffee served by Ran and Yone, as he has previously drugged one of the cups.
  * In the anime, Conan states through Kogoro that Takashi simply avoided the drugged coffee by not drinking it, whereas in the manga, he speculates that Takashi must have marked the cup with a spoon or placed the cup in a recognisable position.
  * In the anime, after Yone is angered by Takashi's cruel remarks and saddened by Reika's passing, she also closes the lifeless Reika's eyes in sign of paying respect to her.
