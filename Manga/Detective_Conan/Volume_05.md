# Detective Conan Volume 05 - The Bandaged Be-header

## Summary

### Case 012 - Bandaged Man Murder Case
#### File 040 - [The Man in Bandages](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_040_-_The_Mysterious..._Bandaged_Man)
Ran's best friend, Sonoko Suzuki, has invited Ran and Conan to her family's villa. When they are on their way to the secluded mountain villa, they are lost and see a man in a coat with a face wrapped in bandages ahead of them on the bridge, but then he disappears. Sonoko greets them at the door and mentions to Ran that her sister has invited some cute guys to the villa. This is their first reunion in two years.

The three men are from the same film club in college as Sonoko's sister. Masaru Ohta is a foreign car broker and was a frequent lead-role actor; Hikori Sumiya is a film magazine editor who used to manage special effects; and Ryoichi Takahashi is an employee at a food product corporation and also was a handy technician who prepares the sets and props. Sonoko's sister, Ayako, was responsible for makeup and wardrobe. Another woman named Chikako Ikeda was the director, the scriptwriter, and the club president. 

Then, they mentioned Atsuko, who already passed away two years ago, and this angers Chikako. To calm the situation, Ayako goes to prepare for dinner while Ryoichi wants to repair the roof before it rains. Chikako angrily leaves the villa to go for a walk, and Hikori tries to warn her about the rain. Sonoko likes Masaru and tells Ran how she admires his coolness. The conversation is interrupted by Masaru and invites Ran to go for a walk in the rain. During the walk, the sudden thunder and lightning scares Ran, causing her to run off alone into the forest. Conan chases after her. Ran is lost in the forest, and the bandaged man is holding an axe behind her.


#### File 041 - [The First Victim!](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_041_-_The_First_Victim.21)
Ran realises someone is behind her, dodges the attack and loses her balance. She screams while the bandaged man is approaching nearer. Conan and Sonoko arrive in time to scare the bandaged man off. When they return to the villa, Sonoko tells everyone of what happened. They decided to call the police, but the phone is disconnected. Ryoichi suspects the bandaged man cuts the phone line and runs towards the bridge. Everyone follows behind, and they see the rope bridge has been cut. 

Chikako thinks the bandaged man created all that mayhem to frighten them. She tells everyone to wait in the house until morning, and then they can cross the mountain after sunrise. When she reaches her room, she finds a letter in front of the door. She says she is tired and will go to bed early without dinner.

During the dinner, Sonoko blames her sister for mentioning Atsuko earlier, causing tension among them. Conan is curious about the story, and Ayako explains that Atsuko was a member of the film club, and she hanged herself in the club room two years ago.

When Ryoichi is on his way into the house from the roof, he sees an unknown figure appear near the window. The group sees the bandaged man carry Chikako away to the forest. Conan immediately grabs the flashlight and rushes to the forest, followed by Masaru, Hikori and Ryoichi. In the forest, they discover Chikako's dismembered body parts.

#### File 042 - [Ran's In Trouble!](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_042_-_Ran.27s_in_Trouble.21)
The men return to the house and report to the others about Chikako's death. The group locks all the doors and windows before they go to sleep and plan to summon the police in the morning. As Conan, Ran, and Sonoko check for the front door, Conan notices Chikako's house slippers are not there. They later find the house slippers at the back door. Conan finds it odd when the bandaged man and Chikako appear at the front after he abducted her at the back door. 

At night, the bandaged man comes into the bedroom and attempts to attack Ran. Conan kicks him off from the bed and later uses the Voice-Changing Bowtie and increases the volume to the maximum level to wake Ran up. Ran screams when she sees the bandaged man, and he flees out the window. Later, Conan finds out that the bandaged man cut the glass of the window near the latch and sneaked into the room. The group decides to stay in the dining room together until morning. Then, Conan notices Ayako has Chikako's choker in her pocket. Ayako says she found it in the hallway when they were chasing the bandaged man and Chikako. However, Conan remembers that seeing Chikako was wearing the choker when the bandaged man took her away.

#### File 043 - [Attack in the Dark!](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_043_-_Attack_in_the_Dark.21)
Lightning strikes and causes a blackout in the house. Ran senses an attack from behind and defends herself. They use the candles and see an axe on the floor. They hear a window-smashing sound on the second floor before the power is restored. Conan finds a clue on the balcony railing and figures out who the murderer is. He plans to use Ran to reveal the murderer, but the tranquilliser hits Sonoko.

#### File 044 - [The Identity of the Murderer](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_044_-_The_Identity_of_the_Murderer.21)

### Case 013 - Lex Vocalist Murder Case
#### File 045 - [The Karaoke Killer!](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_045_-_The_Karaoke_Killer.21)
Sonoko gets the chance to meet Tatsuya Kimura, a singer from a rock band, in person through her father's connections. She invites Ran to the private party, and Ran accepts it.

On Sunday evening, Ran, Sonoko, and Conan enjoy a karaoke session with Tatsuya together with his bandmates Katsumi and Mieko and their manager, Mari. Ran and Sonoko are wondering whether Tatsuya is dating another actress. Tatsuya denies the rumour. Ran and Sonoko are happy to hear Tatsuya's denial while Mieko seems silently angry. 

Tatsuya is heavily drunk and shouts at Mari, who reminds him that there is another talk show that they need to do. He also teases Katsumi's appearance during youth. When the karaoke manager, Go Sumi, comes into the room with snacks, Tatsuya mentions it's sad for their old bandleader to be the owner of such a place. He purposely requested the song "Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer" and taunts Mari for believing in Santa and into singing the song.

Later, when Mieko is singing a farewell song in tears, Conan asks Tatsuya why Mieko is crying. Tatsuya replies that he will be leaving the band after the tour while insulting his other band members. When he hears his song "Bloody Venus" start to play, he refuses to go to the talk show requested by Mari and sings it with all his heart. After he finishes singing, he asks Katsumi to pass him a rice ball. Ran asks Tatsuya whether he is really quitting, and Tatsuya excitedly tells her that he's going for his debut solo album. Before he reveals his new song's title, he coughs up blood after eating the rice ball. He collapses and dies before the ambulance arrives. Conan finds the scent of cyanide on Tatsuya's hand.

#### File 046 - [Suicide or Homicide?](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_046_-_Suicide_or_Homicide.3F)
According to Inspector Megure, Tatsuya most likely died due to potassium cyanide poisoning. Initially, Go Sumii is a suspect because he brought the food into the room, and Katsumi is also suspicious, as he is the one who passed the rice ball to Tatsuya. 

Mari is eliminated as a suspect since she left the room to make a phone call and Go was in the kitchen. Conan, Ran, and Sonoko also can be eliminated as suspects due to lack of motives. 

Later, they find out that the poison is not on the food but on the microphone. Mieko becomes the suspect because she sang before Tatsuya. Megure accuses Mieko of killing Tatsuya for dumping her, but she reveals that Tatsuya rejected her at all because he was in love with another woman and he was waiting for the woman's return. Mari defends Mieko, as Tatsuya was the one who requested her to sing, and he didn't know Mieko would sing before him. 

An officer finds a cream containing potassium cyanide in Tatsuya's car. Inspector Megure concludes it was a suicide. Conan finds the suicide suspicious, as he remembers Tatsuya was excited with his solo debut. There is also no reason for Tatsuya to not have swallowed the poison directly instead of applying it on his right hand. 

When the TV news is reporting on Tatsuya's death, Conan figures out that Tatsuya always throws away his jacket before singing his song. Conan checks with Megure about the acid. Megure tells Conan that the acid was found on Tatsuya’s shirtsleeve by his left elbow. Conan figures out how Tatsuya was poisoned by the person who requested the song ‘Bloody Venus’.

### Case 014 - Conan Edogawa Kidnapping Case
#### File 049 - [An Unfamiliar Visitor](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_049_-_An_Unfamiliar_Visitor)
An unknown woman comes to Ran's house and claims that she is Conan's mother, Fumiyo Edogawa. Conan freaks out because he has never seen the woman before. Once inside the car, Conan asks who she is, and she tells Conan the details about his parents. Later, she threatens him with a gun in the car and promises to bring him to somewhere nice.

Conan worries that she is associated with the men in black. He hits the gas pedal and sends the car into the busy intersection and escapes in the confusion. Alone in the snowy streets, he suspects that the men in black probably know his identity because he used his name to solve the karaoke box murder despite asking the police to keep it anonymous. He is thinking about whether to tell the truth to Ran, but he doesn't want to put her in danger.

The woman takes a phone call from a man with a top hat. She follows his advice and waits outside of Agasa's house for Conan. She knocks Conan out with a chloroform rag before Agasa sees them. After Conan regains consciousness, he finds that he is tied up in the second-floor kitchen of an abandoned house. He hears raised voices arguing about killing him now or following the order from the organisation by keeping him alive for the study of the drug effects. When he peeks through a hole in the door and sees the woman and man, the man turns to look at the room, and Conan is shocked to see that the man is wearing a mask with narrow eyeholes and a big grin.

#### File 050 - [Escape and Pursuit](https://www.detectiveconanworld.com/wiki/Volume_5#File_050_-_Escape_and_Pursuit)
The masked man approaches the room, but Conan pretends that he remains unconscious. He overhears them discussing whether Conan is Shinichi who has shrunk. The man suggests testing a dose of the poison on a contact they're making a deal with the next day. If the drug does indeed shrink people, the man plans to kill both the contact and Conan. The woman disagrees with the plan because she was ordered to have Conan stay alive for the study of the effects of the poison. The man insists on killing Conan to protect the organisation's secrets and threatens the woman with a gun. They confirm the time of the meeting is 1:00 PM the next day.

At night, while the pair sleeps soundly outside the room, Conan carefully breaks a wine bottle left in the room to free himself from his bonds. Although there is snow on the ground, it's still dangerous to jump from the second floor. He plans to hide in the refrigerator but notices wine dripping through the floor and discovers something hidden under some rags.

In the morning, the woman discovers the empty room and declares that Conan must have escaped through the window. The man takes a look outside and declares Conan is still there. He plans to kill Conan and looks everywhere in the room, including the fridge, but it's empty. The pair leaves and decides to find him again after the deal. Once the car has left, Conan climbs out of a trapdoor room on the floor. Conan tries to find the drug, but the pair took everything with them. He tries to find any clues for the meeting and discovers holes in the newspaper when he empties the trash can. He also finds another clue in a nearby calendar, and from the missing letters and calendar, he pieces them together to spell "Beika Hotel 30".

When he arrives at the hotel, Conan finds out that the number "30" is neither a floor nor a tag given by the cloakroom. He overhears a woman mention a numbered parking space and investigates the car parked there to no avail. After the car leaves, a tall man in a mask walks through the garage and inspects the number of the parking stall briefly before returning to the hotel. Conan checks the number again and realises there is a small chalk mark besides the number "30". Conan discreetly follows the tall man to the hotel room and sees the masked man answer the door. As Conan watches from around the corner, the elevator at the end of the hall opens. The woman is walking towards the room, and Conan is trapped between the masked man and the woman.
